"Ending harassment. Empowering women. Taking back the streets."

WHO ARE WE? Anti Street Harassment UK is a grassroots campaign founded in London in 2010. We use social media and guerilla marketing to raise awareness of street harassment in the community. We empower women and campaign supporters to demand change and we lobby authorities to make the streets a safer place for women.

WHAT IS 'STREET HARASSMENT'? Quite simply, it's any form of sexual harassment or sexist behaviour that occurs on the streets - a place where culprits think they can get away with unacceptable behaviour. Ranging from abusive comments yelled across the street to following, groping and assault, we refuse to accept that this stream of abuse is just a fact of life for women. We're taking back the streets.
